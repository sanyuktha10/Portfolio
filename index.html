About Me

I am an Assistant Electrical Engineer at Hayleys Solar, with hands-on experience in designing and commissioning solar PV projects across Sri Lanka. I have successfully delivered over 1.8 MW of solar installations for major industrial and national clients.

Driven by a passion for renewable energy and sustainable power systems, my work focuses on developing reliable and intelligent energy solutions. My final-year project involved designing an optimized hybrid microgrid that achieved over 95% supply reliability for a housing community. I am skilled in PV system design, microgrid optimization, and electrical integration, and I am actively seeking to advance my expertise to help build a sustainable future.
